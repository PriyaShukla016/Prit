Priya & Tapasvi

How we met

February 28th, 2021 was a very special day. Two people, Priya and Tapasvi who were both busy in their lives had their first chat. At around 11:30pm, I(Priya) sent my first text to Tapasvi out of complete boredom and desire to meet someone new. “Hello” I said and there began our story. We talked till 2am that night, I asked questions after questions and he kept replying till finally we went to sleep. Few days passed and we started feeling a strong connection between each other, we started getting attached and slowly developed feelings. One day in the middle of our conversation, Tapasvi confessed his feelings and I very harshly said no even though I did have feelings. I was scared, and I freaked out but I couldn’t deny my feelings either. Almost a month passed, we spent it in confusion. Sometimes we got irritated, once we stopped talking, we even dealt with misunderstandings but eventually our feelings won and we started our relationship.
